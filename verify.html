What Verification Means

A valid result confirms that the JSON content and displayed signature metadata have not changed since they were signed with the private key corresponding to the embedded public key.

It does not independently prove who controlled the key, who received the package, whether an approval was authorized, or whether the package satisfies legal signing requirements.
