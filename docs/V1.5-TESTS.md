# v1.5 Test Plan

## Automated browser coverage

`tests/v15-smoke.spec.js` verifies:

1. schema 1.5, migration registration, panel loading, wrapper installation, and download routing;
2. saving policy stewardship and completing a review;
3. governance-version binding in redacted previews and integrity changes;
4. approved download receipt creation and ledger verification.

`tests/v15-ledger.spec.js` verifies:

1. a valid receipt chain passes verification;
2. changing a stored receipt causes digest verification to fail;
3. inactive, overdue, due-soon, undated, and current policy review states remain distinct.

Earlier smoke suites are retained as regression coverage and now assert schema 1.5 while continuing to exercise their original version-specific APIs.

## Manual release checks

1. Open `meeting.html` directly from the file system.
2. Confirm the app loads without a server and the v1.5 panel appears.
3. Create a recipient policy in the v1.4 panel.
4. Assign a steward, role, risk tier, cadence, and business purpose.
5. Save stewardship and reload the page.
6. Confirm the metadata remains available.
7. Enter reviewer and review-note fields, then mark the policy reviewed.
8. Confirm the recipient policy review date advances.
9. Set review dates representing overdue, due-soon, current, no-date, and inactive states.
10. Confirm the review queue and metrics are correct.
11. Create, approve, and download a recipient-specific external copy.
12. Confirm one receipt appears with approval, recipient, source, package digest, and chain values.
13. Verify the receipt ledger.
14. Export the receipt ledger and governance report.
15. Archive a source record, release an approved copy, and confirm receipt metadata updates the archived record.
16. Confirm both v1.1 external-download buttons route through the approved receipt-producing path.
17. Repeat on HTTPS or localhost and verify the service worker caches all v1.5 assets.

## Regression checks

- Recipient policies still block inactive and overdue use.
- Discussion-note safeguards remain enforced.
- Changing policy or governance metadata invalidates an older approval fingerprint.
- External copies still exclude typed signatures and signature verification data.
- Workspace backup and restore preserve the new storage keys.
- Direct-file operation does not require service-worker support.
- Receipt verification reports locally modified receipt content.
